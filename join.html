TVMK holds regular monthly meetings on the first Tuesday of each month, with special meetings in April for officer installation and the Club's anniversary celebration, and in December for a holiday luncheon. Additionally, optional "Trouble-Shooting Tuesday" meetings provide a space for members to collaborate on solving knitting-related challenges.

Membership is open to anyone with an interest in machine knitting, whether they own a knitting machine or are considering purchasing one. Prospective members are invited to attend up to two meetings before formally joining, with a requirement to attend at least one general meeting.

Members are categorized as "Active" or "Associate," with Active members expected to participate in programs, pay dues, and maintain up-to-date personal information through a bio sheet. Associate members, who are former Active members living outside East Tennessee, retain access to newsletters and other Club activities.

Contact us for more information on joining our next meeting.
